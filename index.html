CHEN

Kim Jongdae, better known by his stage name Chen, is the main vocalist of EXO-M, a sub branch of the super boy band EXO. Known for his

  • sharp jawline
  • high cheek bones
  • and sunny smiles

Chen is the living incarnation of Tadashi Hamada from Big Hero 6. He is EXO’s little sunshine, always looking on the positive side and lifting everyone else’s spirits up with his cute smiles.
